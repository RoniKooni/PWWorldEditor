Tool: Fist

Editor Help

- Move: Use Middle Mouse Scroll to pan around the world.

- Delete: Right Click on any block to remove it instantly.

- Selection: Hold CTRL with Fist tool to select. Use Arrow Keys to move blocks. Click Fist on space to deselect.

- Shapes: Select block in Shapes menu, then drag to draw.

- Water: Water blocks now automatically scroll smoothly to the left.

- Undo: Press CTRL + Z to revert last action.
